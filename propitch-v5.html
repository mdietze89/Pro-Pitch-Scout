Tier Classification System

Our algorithm provides an initial suggestion — the ProPitch admin team makes the final call. The key insight: the same stat line means different things at different ages. A 31-year-old with elite experience is a Tier 2 target; a 22-year-old with the same stats is a Tier 1 prospect.

TIER 1
MLS / Top Professional
Elite domestic pros. MLS, top international, or significant national team experience. Strong stats at this level are expected.
⚡ Age window: Prime 20–28. A 29–31yr old needs elite stats to remain here. 32+ rarely placed T1 regardless of résumé.
80+
ALGO SCORE
TIER 2
USL Championship
Second-division professionals. MLS-level experience but age-adjusted. Strong performers just outside MLS prime window.
⚡ Age insight: A 31–34yr old MLS vet often lands here. A 24yr old USL standout may be elevated to T1 by admin based on upside.
65–79
ALGO SCORE
TIER 3
USL League 1 / NISA
Third-division pros and strong pre-pros. Often college standouts or players ascending from amateur leagues.
⚡ Age insight: Young players (18–22) with high stats are flagged as upside targets. Older players with T3 stats stay T3–4.
50–64
ALGO SCORE
TIER 4
USL League 2 / NPSL
Highest quality amateur/semi-pro. Key development ground. Generates real professional talent consistently.
⚡ Age insight: Youth players (U21) here are tracked as long-term investments by T2–3 clubs.
35–49
ALGO SCORE
TIER 5
MISL / Youth / Development
Indoor specialists, academy players, emerging youth. Age and trajectory are the primary scouting factors here.
⚡ Age insight: A 16–19yr old in T5 with elite raw stats may be admin-bumped to T4 purely on potential.
<35
